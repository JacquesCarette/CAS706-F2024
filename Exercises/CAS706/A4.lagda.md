
Exercise: ExistsNegImpNegForAll (1 point)
Existence of negation implies negation of universal.
```agda
∃¬-implies-¬∀ : ∀ {A : Set} {B : A → Set}
  → ∃[ x ] (¬ B x)
    --------------
  → ¬ (∀ x → B x)
∃¬-implies-¬∀ ∃¬B = {!!}
```

The converse cannot be proved in intuitionistic logic.
